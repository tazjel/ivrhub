About

IVRHub makes it easy to conduct surveys and collect data using mobile phones. There are no apps to install, and there's no harware to deploy. Because the questions are voice-based, there are no literacy requirements for enumerators and the surveys can be in any language.

If your respondents can make phone calls, they can use IVRHub. Here's how it works:

  1. The survey-designer creates an account and begins building a form. Form questions can have custom audio prompts from an uploaded file or the web. We can also use text-to-speech software in several languages.

  2. After setting up the questions, the designer publishes the form and receives a unique calling code.

  3. Survey respondents are given the form's calling code. They can either directly call the IVRHub service and enter the code to begin a response, or they can text the code to IVRHub and we will call them back.

  4. The respondents complete the questions using their keypad or by speaking their answers.

  5. Answers are available on IVRHub for the form designer to review. The voice responses can be played back for the designer to transcribe.

  6. Data may be exported for further analysis.
